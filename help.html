Junos Pulse VPN Client

Step 1: Download and Install Junos Pulse

You can find the Junos Pulse client on the Apple App Store. Download and install it, then follow the instructions below.

Step 2: Launch Junos Pulse and Enable it

Click the "Enable" button

Step 3: Set Up Configuration

Click on "Configuration" and then on "Add New Configuration."

Step 4: Enter Information

Enter "VPN" as the name and "https://yourvpn.com" as the URL. Leave the field labeled Certificate as it is.

Step 5: Connect to the Configuration named "VPN"

Upon saving the new configuration you will be returned to the screen below. Click "Connect".

Step 6: Enter in your SecureID Username and Token

These were issued to you by your IT staff. If you do not have one, you must contact them to obtain one. Please note that your SecureID token is a combination of a numeric passcode chosen by you and the rotating numeric sequence generated by your RSA SecurID token generator. Click "Sign In" after you have entered your credentials. In a few moments, you should be connected to the VPN. At that time you may close the Junos Pulse client interface and use your mobile device as you normally would. You don’t need to do anything special to access the network once you have connected to the VPN. If your VPN session becomes disconnected or times out, you will need to re?authenticate following steps 5 and 6 in this guide.
